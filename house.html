Our House

The Chi Phi Chapter House boasts the first steel-reinforced concrete frame in the northeast.

It was originally built by an architect as his own personal residence.

The house has 10 rooms where the Brothers live. There are 2 singles, 6 doubles, and 2 triples. It also has a full kitchen, basement, living and dining area.

The house phone number is 201-876-1824.
